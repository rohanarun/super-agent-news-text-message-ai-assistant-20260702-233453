How a text becomes a workflow

1. You text a request

“Find the cheapest flight,” “update my spreadsheet,” or “check yesterday’s analytics.”

2. Super uses a computer

The agent opens browsers and apps, logs in where you’ve approved access, and performs the task.

3. Results + follow‑ups

You get a summary by text and can reply with corrections or next steps.

4. computer-use cache

Repeated tasks reuse the same computer-use cache, so future runs are faster and cheaper.
